Beef Lasagne

Description

A quick and easy beef lasagne that's extra cheesy and ready to eat in just 1 hour! And best of all, a time saver, as there's absolutely no need to make a bechamel sauce! The perfect midweek family dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 500g beef mince
  • 65g tomato paste
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • 2 cups beef stock
  • 2 tsp dried italian parsley
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 250g mozzarella cheese
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • 375g lasagne sheets

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan or deep frying pan. Add the diced onion and minced garlic and cook until soft and translucent.
  2. Add the beef mince, breaking the clumps apart with a spoon, and cook until browned.
  3. Stir through the tomato paste, canned tomatoes, beef stock and herbs. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bring to the boil and then cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees celsius (fan-forced).
  6. Lightly grease a large baking dish.
  7. Spoon ½ cup of the bolognese sauce into the bottom of the prepared dish.
  8. Layer with the fresh lasagne sheets and ¼ of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat with the meat, lasagne sheets and mozzarella until you have 4 layers, finishing with a mozzarella layer.
  10. Sprinkle over the grated parmesan cheese.
  11. Bake in the oven for 30-40 minutes or until cooked through.
  12. Leave to stand for 5 minutes before serving.
