Potato Bake

Description

If you're looking for the perfect potato side dish, this Easy Cheesy Potato Bake recipe has you covered. Meltingly tender, creamy scalloped potatoes laced with bacon, thyme and cheese all baked in a comforting sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1.4kg potatoes
  • 75g bacon, roughly diced
  • 1 large clove garlic, crushed
  • 3 large spring onions
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup plain flour
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 cups grated cheddar (200g)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C
  2. Peel the potatoes and use a sharp knife to slice them very thinly (no more than 3-4mm thick). Place them in a large saucepan of water, and bring to the boil. Boil for 2 minutes, remove from heat and drain. Set aside.
  3. Combine the cream and milk in a small jug and set aside.
  4. Add the oil and bacon to the same pan and saute until just starting to brown.
  5. Add the onions and garlic and saute for a minute to soften a little before adding the butter.
  6. Once the butter has melted add the flour. Stir well and cook for 30 seconds.
  7. Slowly drizzle in the stock while continuing to stir until the flour is totally dissolved into it.
  8. Turn the heat down to low-medium, slowly add the cream and milk while whisking. Immediately add the thyme, salt and pepper. Keep stirring until it starts to thicken and bubble a little.
  9. Place about ⅓ cup of sauce in the base of a casserole dish and spread around.
  10. Now add layers, ⅓ of each in this order: potatoes, sauce, cheese (so you finish with cheese)
  11. Bake for 45-50 minutes until bubbling and just starting to turn golden on top.
  12. If you want it a little browner on top, place it under an overhead grill / broiler for 2-3 minutes. No longer as the heat may cause the sauce to curdle.
